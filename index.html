About Carbon Footprint

Carbon footprint refers to the total amount of greenhouse gases emitted into the atmosphere by an individual, organization, event, or product. It is usually measured in terms of carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2e) and includes emissions from energy use, transportation, and other activities.

The goal of this calculator is to help you understand your personal carbon footprint and find ways to reduce it. Click on the "Calculate" tab to estimate your carbon footprint.
